About the Book

Game Changer, by Neal Shusterman, is a book about a boy called Ash who plays football in his American school. This is the story of Ash and his experience travelling through different universes that are each similar but not like his own. For example, in one of them stop signs are blue, but in the next the Supreme Court doesn't rule down segregation and black people don't have rights.
